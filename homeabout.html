Home & About Pages:

These are the 2 pages your viewers will be able to see within the app. The home view is your scroll.xml file, all the information you need to change on the homescreen is located here.
Only change the text located on the: android:text area don't mess with anything else unless you know what you're doing. There are a dark version & a light version of this file.


With this file only change the text in the Your Info Area. I'll tell you everything that is changeable. Now it'll underline read don't worry you didn't mess up that is explained in your next step.
Anything that has yournamehere change to your name don't change anything before the _. Now to get your own avatar to show up change this android:src="@drawable/yourimage", only change yourimage to the name of your avatar image. Now make sure your image isn't that big the app will resize it. The bigger you have your image the longer the app will take to load because it has to resize the image to fit.
There is one more area you can change if you'd like. Scroll to the top until you see this line of code android:text="This dashboard was made by:" you can change that text to your liking.
